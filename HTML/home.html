How to Play WordMaster

WordMaster is a word-guessing game where you need to figure out the hidden word using the clue provided. You have multiple attempts to guess the correct word, and after each guess, you'll receive color-coded feedback to help you narrow down the answer.

Read the clue carefully and start guessing! Use the on-screen keyboard to enter your letters. The game will guide you with instant feedback after each attempt.

Color Feedback System

S
Green: The letter is correct and in the right position.
H
Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
K
Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.
